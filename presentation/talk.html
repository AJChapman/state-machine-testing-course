Command

An instruction, the workhorse.

  • Pre/Post conditions
  • Input generation

Model

Your expectations regarding the 'state' of the world.

The general process

  • Pre-conditions are checked
  • Input is generated
  • Action is executed
  • Model is updated
  • Post-conditions are checked
